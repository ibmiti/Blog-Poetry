Blog Entry: April
Hi, Hello world.. Welcome. These past few year's have been a roller coaster, a hike a fall. I've gotten married to the girl of my dream's, I finally entered into the corporate world; which has given me financial stability & plan for the future. I can help those around me. Ideal time it would seem to me to be, But Ideal.. is never truly ever ideal, that's the deal.. it gives and it takes, bends for gleeful surprises but then breaks beyond. But no matter what, I am in love with life, and I do not; nor do I think I ever will truly understand it. But that's the beauty of it all.. am I right?
again Welcome and I hope you enjoy the dive within my thought's for they are deep like well spring.

In this blog of mine you will be introduced to my life as it currently is, and how it promises to be. Thank you for your time.
